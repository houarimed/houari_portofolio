A Bit About Me

Hello, I'm Houari

Hello! I'm Houari from Algeria. I enjoy discovering new stories, learning languages, discussing fantasy worlds, exploring history, and meeting people from different cultures.

This website is my personal corner of the internet — a place where you can learn about my interests and, if you feel like it, reach out and say hello.

I believe the best conversations start with shared curiosity. Whether it's debating magic systems, recommending a book, swapping phrases in a new language, or just chatting about something interesting — I'm always happy to connect.

Curious Imaginative Friendly Thoughtful Open-Minded Lifelong Learner

"Every book is a door. Every language is a window. Every person I meet comes from a world I haven't fully explored yet — and that's exactly what keeps me curious."
